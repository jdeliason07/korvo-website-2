About Korvo AI

We are a student-owned, student-run AI software company offering the latest in computer science and business management at a fraction of traditional costs.

Our Story

A different kind of AI company

Korvo AI is a fully student-owned and -operated company started in 2025 by River Anatasio, a computer science major. In early 2026, River sold the company to Jack Eliason, a business major.

This has given Korvo AI the unique position of being built on the foundations of the latest education in computer science and framed by the latest education in business management.

By hiring student developers, Korvo AI can provide the most up-to-date software at a significantly lower cost, giving our clients the best service at an increased rate of ROI.

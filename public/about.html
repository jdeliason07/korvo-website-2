About Korvo AI

A student-owned company built on the latest education in computer science and business — delivering cutting-edge AI at a fraction of traditional costs.

Our Story

A different kind of AI company

Korvo AI was founded in 2025 by River Anatasio, a computer science major at Brigham Young University, with a simple idea: the latest AI education shouldn't stay in the classroom.

In early 2026, Jack Eliason — a BYU business student and multi-business owner — acquired Korvo AI, bringing business strategy and entrepreneurial drive to match the company's technical foundation.

This combination gives Korvo AI a unique position: software built on the latest computer science education, wrapped in the latest business management thinking. The result is an AI company that's both technically sharp and commercially grounded.

How It Works

Win-win pricing — we succeed when you succeed

Traditional SaaS charges you a flat monthly fee regardless of results. Our model ties your subscription to your actual Korvo AI ROI. If the software isn't delivering, your bill reflects that.

FAQ

Pricing questions, answered

What exactly is "ROI-based pricing"? +
After implementation, we track the measurable value your Korvo AI tools generate — saved staff hours, additional bookings, reduced no-shows, etc. Your monthly subscription is calculated as a percentage of that monthly ROI figure, not a flat rate. If your AI isn't producing, your bill drops accordingly.
How do you measure ROI? +
We agree on a measurement framework before we start — typically based on metrics like calls handled, appointments booked, staff hours saved, or revenue attributed to AI-driven leads. We track these monthly and report transparently.
Why is there an implementation fee? +
Research, building, and deploying a custom AI system takes real time and expertise. The implementation fee covers that upfront work. Monthly subscriptions then remain tied to ongoing value — so our long-term incentives are fully aligned with yours.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. We don't believe in locking clients into contracts when the ROI isn't clear. If it's not working for your business, you can cancel your subscription anytime.
Is the free consultation really free? +
Completely. No credit card, no obligation. It's an honest conversation about your business and where AI might help. If it's not a fit, we'll tell you — that's just how we build trust.
